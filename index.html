I am a 4th year PhD student of Advanced Materials Lab at Department of Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University, advised by Professor L. Catherine Brinson. I received my B.S. degree in Aerospace Engineering from University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, Indiana in 2012.

Research
